Enter the Darklands

A dark fantasy expansion for Savage Worlds. Inspired by the Witcher, Kingdom Come Deliverance, Battle Brothers, Banner Saga, Wheel of Time and Black Company.

Required Core Rules

Enter the Darklands is an expansion for the Savage Worlds Adventure Edition roleplaying game. Please support the original creators by purchasing SWADE from Pinnacle's official store.
